A New Musical · In Development

A young woman refuses to trade her truth for a fairytale ending.

In a bleak 19th-century fishing town, 18-year-old Gabi escapes the grey through her imagination — and the three classic princesses who bicker inside her head. When a world-famous fairytale contest opens its doors to men only, she sells her late mother's wing necklace and writes her way to the Big City.
